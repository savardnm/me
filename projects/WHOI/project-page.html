Description

Over the summer of 2020, I started working with Woods Hole Oceanographic Institute (WHOI) by creating models, assemblies, and renders of piping systems. Models were made based on engineering specifications and dimensions.

I created these models using Autodesk Inventor. I used Parametric modelling to generate families of parts using excel-driven dimensions. This allowed me to create many versions of a single part, without having to re-model individual pieces. This workflow also allows me to make iterative changes to models and assemblies, without creating new versions.

This page is a gallery of some of the models and images I created.
